About

myself

I am an incoming senior at UC San Diego pursuing a major in Computer Engineering. I am interested in data analysis, software developing, project managing and leadership. I actively seek ways to challenge myself to develop more relevant industrial experiences in these fields and use these experiences to enrich my career as a computer engineer and eventually become a project manager to lead the team. I am driven to influence and inspire the lives of people around me. I joined student council in both high school and college to challenge the status quo of the student policies. In college, I helped people in the local La Jolla community through both Circle K International and Sixth College student council to make a difference in their life. In my leisure time, I usually work with different engineering projects and internships. I gained valuable experiences in 3D printers, soldering, board assembly, coding and data analysis. Apart from my technical skills, I love to lift in the gym because I love to look sharp and fit. In an age where life is dominated by digital media I believe bringing design to life with code is essential. So, I major in computer engineering to study both coding in the software part and board design in the hardware part. During my recent internship with UC medical center, when I am looking at the energy efficiency of the hospital, I realize there are definite ways to save energy. I will be working on a project that implements of a data monitor program to bring the visualization of energy usage of each facility to live. Through computer engineering, I want to enrich my experiences in data analysis, software developing, project managing and leadership to influence others in a positive way. Feel free to contact me with opportunities or just to chat via email: yjpei@ucsd.edu
